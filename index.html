Key Insights & Recommendations

A 45-day media plan was projected with an initial daily spend of ₹5,000.
Actual spends were approximately 47% lower than planned due to multiple technical and operational challenges.
During the initial phase, only ~30% of overall ad viewers were reaching the website.
Website speed was identified as a major factor affecting landing rates.
Website optimizations were implemented, including removing unnecessary apps and converting high-resolution images to WebP format.
Post-optimization, website performance improved significantly, with landing rates increasing to 70–75%.
Based on these improvements, the daily ad budget was adjusted to ₹2,000 for few days to ensure stable performance.
Campaigns had several pauses due to work in progress on website, website speed and insufficient funds in the ad account.
No purchases were recorded in November; conversions began in December, though consistency remained a challenge.
There were inventory issues which showed most products as Out Of Stock, leading to rising drop-offs from website as visitors selecting products from the collection page would see OOS in multiple selections.
Stock levels were updated by EOD on 12th December.
From 13th December, fresh campaigns were launched post inventory correction.
These campaigns resulted in 8 purchases in December, delivering a ROAS of 0.77.
Multiple additional optimizations were implemented during this period.
Some improvements are still work in progress, including the development of a dedicated Ready to Ship (RTS) collection page to better attract users toward in-stock products.
The performance metrics mentioned above are derived solely from the Meta platform.
AI-generated product images were uploaded on collection pages to increase visual appeal and drive higher user engagement.
Product descriptions are being updated to clearly differentiate between Ready to Ship (RTS) and Made-to-Order products.
Description updates are currently work in progress and will be completed in phases to improve clarity and reduce user confusion.

Key Challenges & Resolutions

Website Performance Issues

Initial landing rate: Only 30% of ad viewers reached the website due to slow loading speeds.

Resolution: Implemented optimizations including removal of unnecessary apps and conversion to WebP image format. Post-optimization landing rates improved to 70-75%.
Inventory Management

Multiple products showed as "Out of Stock," causing significant drop-offs from collection pages.

Resolution: Stock levels updated by EOD December 12th. Fresh campaigns launched from December 13th resulted in 8 purchases.
Campaign Interruptions

Multiple pauses due to website work-in-progress, speed issues, and insufficient ad account funds.

Action Taken: Daily budget adjusted to ₹2,000 during optimization period to ensure stable performance.

Optimizations Implemented

  • Product descriptions being updated to differentiate RTS vs Made-to-Order products
  • Website speed optimizations significantly improved user experience
  • Ready to Ship (RTS) collection page development in progress
