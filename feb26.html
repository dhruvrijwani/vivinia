💡 Efficiency Insight

Despite spending 24% less than projected (₹1,17,635 vs ₹1,55,000), the campaign exceeded the ROAS target of 1.00x, achieving 1.19x. This reflects a significant improvement in campaign efficiency driven by audience optimisations and creative learnings carried forward from January's second half.

💡 Key Insights & Recommendations
  • 📌 Scale Opportunity: With ROAS now above 1x, this is a good opportunity to gradually increase the daily budget to scale revenue further. While scaling, closely monitor frequency and CTR to avoid creative fatigue and performance drop.
  • 📌 Audience Strategy Working: The South audience segmentation is performing well — we are targeting product-specific categories like South Indian temple jewellery in top-tier cities. Additionally, we have a separate campaign targeting top-tier cities overall, which is also delivering strong results.
  • 📌 Occasion Campaign Insight: We tested a Valentine's Day offer on specific products only, but the performance was not strong and delivered just 1 purchase. It is recommended to do a sitewide offer instead of product-specific discounts to improve traction and overall conversions.
  • 📌 Influencer & UGC Creatives: Competitors are actively using influencer collaboration ads. It is recommended to create influencer/UGC-based video-led ads, as this can significantly improve engagement, CTR, and conversion performance.
  • 📌 Post Boosting Strategy (March): In March, we executed post boosting to gain additional reach. Going forward, it is recommended to continue post boosting consistently to maintain higher visibility, improve brand recall, and support overall campaign performance.
  • 📌 Bridal Appointment – Lead Gen Campaign: This month, we tested a lead generation campaign for bridal appointments. We received 3 leads, out of which 1 was a warm and relevant lead. The total spend was approximately ₹10K.
  • 📌 WhatsApp Lead Gen – Bridal Appointments: We also tested a WhatsApp lead generation campaign for bridal appointments. Initially, it generated some junk leads; however, after campaign optimisation, we started receiving better-quality enquiries. It is important to note that WhatsApp campaigns naturally attract some low-intent or junk enquiries.
  • 📌 Platform Note: All performance metrics mentioned above are derived solely from the Meta platform.
