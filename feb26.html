Key Insights & Recommendations
  • Competitors are actively using influencer collaboration ads. It is recommended to create influencer/UGC-based video-led ads, as this can significantly improve engagement, CTR, and conversion performance. UGC Home Styling Videos: Develop authentic user-generated content featuring customer reviews, testimonials, and creator partnership videos. This approach builds credibility through real customer experiences and third-party validation, making our products more relatable and trustworthy.
  • It is recommended to continue post boosting consistently going forward to maintain higher visibility, improve brand recall, and support overall campaign performance.
  • It is recommended to add an additional platform, namely Google Ads, to improve performance. Google Ads can help capture high-intent users who are actively searching for the product, increase overall reach, and drive more qualified traffic and conversions, which can help improve orders and overall revenue.
  • Continue scaling campaigns and creative formats that demonstrated strong results in February.
  • Improve the catalog by adding model images or using white/black backgrounds to showcase the products more clearly and make them more visually appealing.
  • Use WhatsApp and email to retarget abandoned cart users and existing customers, highlighting the discounts and reminding them about the items left in their cart to encourage completion of purchase.
  • It was also observed in February that even RTS products were not being delivered on time, which creates a poor customer experience. It is important to improve delivery timelines to ensure better customer satisfaction and reduce potential order cancellations.
Key Achievements & Improvements
✓ ROAS Crossed 1x for the First Time: February achieved a 1.19x ROAS — a direct result of audience optimisations and creative learnings from January's second half.
✓ Orders Nearly Doubled: Grew from 11 orders in January to 21 in February (+91%), demonstrating the lasting impact of the audience testing strategy.
✓ Revenue More Than Doubled: Revenue grew from ₹64,125 to ₹1,39,819 (+118%) while ad spend decreased — confirming improved campaign efficiency.
✓ AOV Improved: Average Order Value rose from ₹5,730 to ₹6,658 (+16%), indicating customers are purchasing higher-value items or multiple products per order.
✓ Cost Per Order Improved: Dropped from ~₹11,111 in January to ~₹5,602 in February — a 50% reduction in acquisition cost.
