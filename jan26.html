Key Insights & Recommendations

Actual spends were approximately 21% lower than planned, primarily due to multiple technical and operational challenges.
During performance analysis, we identified that the SPF Product Filter app was negatively impacting website speed on collection pages. This slowdown was causing delayed page loads and contributing to a drop in landing page views. To resolve this, we removed the SPF Product Filter app and restored Shopify's native filtering system. As a result, collection pages now load faster, improving overall site performance and enhancing the user experience, which is expected to positively impact landing page engagement.
From 8th–10th Jan, we observed a significant drop in the Click to LPV %, so we decided to pause the campaign by 10th Jan EOD to focus on website optimization. The campaign was restarted on 14th Jan.
Additionally, we changed the pixel on 16th Jan to refresh the learning, and the campaign was relaunched on the same day. The reason for changing the pixel was to refresh learning, as we were receiving very low CPMs and CPC under ₹1.5. Post this change, we started seeing improvements in overall numbers and performance.
We also added the RTS tag on the website to attract and convert more customers.
From 19th Jan onwards, we tested different audience types, and they performed well—leading to an increase in the number of orders and overall revenue. ROAS improved to 1.20 (19th–31st Jan), compared to 0.18 (1st–18th Jan).
Currently, since products go out of stock immediately once sold, it is recommended to keep at least the latest 3–4 Inventory live to avoid wasted ad spend and improve campaign stability.
The performance metrics mentioned above are derived solely from the Meta platform.
It has also been observed that some distributors are offering higher discounts than our official pricing, which may be impacting conversions and purchase decisions. It is recommended to align with distributors and ensure they do not offer higher discounts than us.
Competitors are actively using influencer collaboration ads, it is recommended that we also create influencer/UGC-based video-led ads, as this can significantly help improve engagement, CTR, and overall conversion performance.

Key Achievements & Improvements

✓ Website Speed Optimization: Removed SPF Product Filter app and restored Shopify's native filtering, significantly improving collection page load times and user experience.
✓ Pixel Refresh Strategy: Changed pixel on 16th Jan to refresh learning phase, resulting in improved CPMs, CPC, and overall campaign performance.
✓ Audience Testing Success: From 19th Jan onwards, different audience types were tested with excellent results. ROAS improved dramatically to 1.20 (19th–31st Jan) from 0.18 (1st–18th Jan).
✓ RTS Implementation: Added RTS (Ready to Ship) tags on website to better attract and convert customers looking for immediate purchases.
